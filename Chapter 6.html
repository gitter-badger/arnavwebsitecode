Chapter 6: Solar eclipse and Lunar eclipse

Solar eclipse

Is when the Moon is between the

Earth and the Sun. Moon blocks Sun’s

light to reach till Earth. And the back part of

moon reflects on the Earth. And the moon gets light.



Lunar eclipse

Is when the Earth is between the Moon

and the Sun. Earth block Sun’s light to go to

the Moon. And the back part of Earth

reflects on the Moon. And the Earth gets light.
