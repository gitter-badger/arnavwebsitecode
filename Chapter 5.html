Dwarf Planet

Ok so in this topic we will learn about dwarf planets.

Dwarf planets are planet that are small.

There are many dwarf planets but I will only tell you 3 large dwarf planets.



Pluto was known as ninth planet. But it became smaller

so scientist consider that Pluto is dwarf planet. And Pluto has moons called

Nix, Hydra, Styx, Charon, and Kerberos.



Ceres is the 2nd smallest dwarf planet in our

Solar system. Its color is gray

like Mercury and a craters.



Eris has same size as Pluto. It has one moon

named Dyssomnia. It is also first dwarf planet in our Solar system. Eris is

almost same size a Pluto Its color is gray same

as Ceres and Mercury.
