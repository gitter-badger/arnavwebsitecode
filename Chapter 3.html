Chapter 3: Inner planets and Outer planets

Inner planets and Outer planets

Are planets closer to the sun. And the Earth is a terrestrial planet

where we are living. Lastly Inner planets or Terrestrial planets have few

moons or no moons.



Outer planets or Jovian Planets

Are planets that are made with gas and they are bigger than the terrestrial planets. Some of

them also have different types of strong storms. There are 2 parts

of Outer planets or Jovian Planets. First 2 planets are called as Gas Giants.

They are Jupitar and Saturn. And other 2 planets

are called as Ice Gaints They are Uranus and Neptune.
